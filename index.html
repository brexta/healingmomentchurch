Greetings to you beloved. On behalf of the entire Healing Moment Team, we welcome you to our ministry page. We are glad you are here.

The core of our ministry mission is healing-evangelism and leadership development. This is broken down into the following parts: healing sick bodies, evangelizing the unsaved, raising godly marriages, empowering Christians in business, and raising leaders for Christ in all fields of life.

We value people of all colour, tribe and language because we see them with the eyes of love. We believe that everyone has a purpose from God, and our main goal is to help them discover and fulfil it excellently. That is why our motto is “Where Destinies Emerge.”

Apostle Julius P. Isabirye & Pr. Nancy Isabirye
