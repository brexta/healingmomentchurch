The Four-Fold Ministry Model

Everything we do flows from a 4-dimensional God-given calling. The four pillars together form a complete life — whole in body, family, finances, and soul.

Body
🙌

Healing the Sick

Contending for people's physical bodies — the devil has no place in them. Healing attracts the lost and opens doors to salvation and leadership.

  • Monthly Healing Experience Service
  • Prayer for the sick at every service
  • Hospital ministry (coming soon)
  • Healing evangelism outreaches
Family
💍

Marriage & Family

Restoring people to value one another. Strong marriages reduce crime, perversion, and generational pain. Pr. Nancy leads this arm.

  • Marriage counselling & teaching
  • Family & couples conferences
  • Pre-marital counselling
  • Children's Church (in development)
Finance
📈

Business & Prosperity

Empowering people financially. Money is a tool to finance the gospel — raising kingdom builders who steward wealth for God.

  • Business mentorship & wisdom
  • Leadership development
  • Education scholarship fund
  • The Effective Tithe teaching
Soul
🌍

Evangelism & Leadership

Contending for souls and raising leaders who bring order, clarity, and God's vision to every sphere of society.

  • Door-to-door & crusade outreaches
  • Secondary school reach project
  • Prayer evangelism week
  • Pr. Nancy leads evangelism team
