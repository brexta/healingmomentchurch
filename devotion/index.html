How to Fight and Win II
“Fight the good fight of the faith; lay hold of the eternal life to which you were summoned and [for which] you confessed the good confession [of faith] before many witnesses.”
1 Timothy 6:12 (AMP)
In Christ, we have only one fight - it is the good fight of faith. The good fight of faith is the conflict between the life promised us in God’s word, and the realities we see everyday. It is the challenge to manifest all that Christ has made available to you through His death, burial and resurrection.

In part one, I taught that to win this fight, the first thing you need is to fight using the word of God. You must know the promises of God and stand on them in faith to declare the changes you need in your life. If you didn’t read it, kindly get it and it will bless you.

Secondly, you must fight using the Name of Jesus. Jesus said, “And whatsoever ye shall ask in my name, that will I do, that the Father may be glorified in the Son. If ye shall ask any thing in my name, I will do it.” John 14:13-14 (KJV)

There is power in the Name of Jesus. It is the channel for accessing all that God has available for you. That is why Jesus boldly said that whatever you ask for in His Name, will be given to you.

The Name of Jesus is also your platform for exercising the divine authority and power you have received in Christ. In that Name, you can cast out devils, minister healing to the sick and receive divine protection from all that wants to harm you (Mark 16:17-18). It is the Name that elevates you above the devil and all the forces of darkness.

Acts 4:12 reveals that it is the only Name by which we are saved. Whoever calls upon the Name of the Lord, can be saved or delivered.

I want you to note that unlike under the Old Testament where various names of God were given, under the New Testament, we have been given only one Name - it is the Name of Jesus. All the different names of God were partial representations of the character and power of God. However, the full expression of God’s character and power was revealed in Christ. So, His Name is enough to represent all the names of God given under the Old Testament.

Colossians 2:9 reveals that in Jesus, dwells all the fullness of the God-head bodily. So, when you call on the Name of Jesus, all of heaven backs you up. It is the Name vested with all the authority and power of the God-Head. Hallelujah!

That is why Philippians 2:9-11 says that at the sound of that Name, every knee MUST BOW of things in heaven, things in the earth and things under the earth. When you fight using the Name of Jesus, you have the backing of all heaven. It is the Name you are meant to use in prayer to enforce the will of God in your life. It is the Name that provokes the release of God’s power in every situation and causes miracles to happen. It is the Name above all Names! Glory to Jesus forever!
