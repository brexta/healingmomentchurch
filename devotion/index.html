Isabirye YOUR INHERITANCE IN CHRIST Christ himself carried our sins in his body to the cross, so that we might die to sin and live for righteousness. It is by his wounds that you have been healed. 1 PETER 2:24 (GNT) Faith is a result of having a clear knowledge of God's word. Until the word of God is made clear to you, it is difficult to have faith. One of the main purposes of God's word is to reveal to us our inheritance in Christ. An inheritance refers to that which is rightfully yours by law. It is your appointed possession by law because of the unique relationship you have with someone. When Christ died for us, He not only redeemed us but also purchased for us an inheritance in God. In Psalm 103:2, David prophetically called this inheritance 'benefits'. He said, "Bless the Lord oh my soul and forget none of his benefits." He helps us understand that there are many benefits for being connected to Jesus Christ as your Lord and Savior. These benefits are our inheritance; they are our rightfully legal possession through faith in Jesus Christ. Verse 3 says, "Who forgives all your sins and heals all your diseases". These two are our first benefits or redemptive benefits. All our sins are forgiven and all our disease are healed. This is the same message echoed in 1Peter 2:24. Christ died for us to obtain righteousness and by His wounds we were healed of all sickness and disease. As a believer in Christ, forgiveness of sins and divine healing are your lawful inheritance. They are rightfully yours because of your connection to Christ as your Lord and Savior. This means that beyond having faith for forgiveness and healing, God is always ready to forgive all your sins and heal your body of every disease because of your relationship with Christ. The death of Christ is the foundation you should always stand upon and be assured of forgiveness for all your sins and healing for all sickness. By the death of Jesus, the court of heaven already ruled that you are an heir of righteousness and divine healing. So, all you have to do is claim your inheritance through faith, and you will have it, in Jesus Name. That is te knowledge that makes faith for forgiveness of sins and divine healing simple. It is your inheritance, in Jesus Name! MEDITATION: 1Peter 2:24, Isaiah 53:4-5 HEALING INSIGHT: As a believer in Christ, forgiveness of sins and divine healing are your lawful inheritance. They are rightfully yours because of your connection to Christ as your Lord and Savior.
