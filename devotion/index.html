Monday, July 6th, 2026 · Apostle Julius P. Isabirye
“And Caleb stilled the people before Moses, and said, Let us go up at once, and possess it; for we are well able to overcome it. But the men that went up with him said, We be not able to go up against the people; for they are stronger than we. And they brought up an evil report of the land which they had searched unto the children of Israel, saying, The land, through which we have gone to search it, is a land that eateth up the inhabitants thereof; and all the people that we saw in it are men of a great stature. And there we saw the giants, the sons of Anak, which come of the giants: and we were in our own sight as grasshoppers, and so we were in their sight.”
Numbers 13:30-33 (KJV)
When God makes a promise to you, the next phase is for you to believe that promise. Believing what God says to you, is the key to possessing what He promises. Your faith is what unlocks the potentials and possibilities in God’s promises to you.

In our opening scripture, we see that though God had made a promise to give Israel the promised land, they did not believe it. The doubt was mainly caused by the evil report of unbelief brought back by the ten spies.

Unbelief and doubt disconnect you from what God has for you. They are Satan’s tools for hindering you from walking in the promises of God.

When God tells you that the land is yours and you say “I can’t possess it”, you make His word of promise ineffective in your life. Faith is the key to cooperating and working with God. When you believe, you are saying to God, “I choose to agree with you”.

Doubt is disagreeing with God and His word. It is thinking, speaking and acting contrary to what He desires for you. The challenge of faith is to take God at His word inspite of the obstacles you see. If God says you are taking the land, don’t consider the giants. If He says by His wounds you were healed, don’t consider the symptoms.

Understand that whatever you exalt, is what you worship. What you worship is what you truly believe in. Exalting God and His word above the contrary situations, is a sign of worship to Him. It pleases Him and provokes His power to work for you. This is the challenge of faith. Glory to Jesus!
