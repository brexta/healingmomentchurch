Knowing the Right Path to Take
“Trust in the Lord with all your heart; do not depend on your own understanding. Seek his will in all you do, and he will direct your paths.”
Proverbs 3:5-6 (NLT)
When I perceived that my time for getting married had come, I asked one of my friends to pray with me about the matter. His answer shocked me. He said, “Is God the One going to get married? Just choose a girl you like and marry them?”

As shocking as this was to me, I have discovered that it is the thinking of many young people even in church. However, marriage is delicate and very important that it requires God’s help to make the right decision. What my friend was advising was for me to rely on myself as I choose a mate. However, our opening scripture reveals the opposite.

God wants to be involved in all your decisions including choosing a mate to marry. Until you learn to depend on God in all your decision making, it will be impossible for you to stop making bad decisions.

Leaning on your own understanding instead of leaning on God and His wisdom, is the reason for poor decision making and failure. Understand that God loves you and He has the best interests at heart towards you. All His guidance and counsel is to help you succeed and never fail. This is what should make you humble enough to trust Him and depend on Him in all things.

The best decisions are made in God and not outside Him. It is through His wisdom that we are saved from making wrong choices in relationships, business, career, and ministry.

Note also that every decision made in God has His protection and backing. Every decision made outside Him, has to be backed by you and your strength. God is not obligated to preserve or protect what He never started but guards jealously everything He was part of from the beginning.

To know the right way to take, you must depend on God to guide you. Trust Him to continually guide your steps in all things because He is all knowing. “Seek his will in all you do, and He will direct your paths.” That is the ancient wisdom for always making the right decisions in life, in Jesus Mighty Name!
